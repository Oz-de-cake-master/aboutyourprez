About Me

> My name is Alex Joshua Espinoza, and as of January 25th, I am 18 years old. I've had a childhood alot of people would consider unique, one distant from the influences of social media or modernized technology. Growing up alongside 2 of my brothers and my job as a waiter, I've grown to enjoy the little things in life and live in the moment while carefully planning my future. Spending a decent amount of my life in solitude, I've grown accustomed to the intricacies of old computers and consoles like the Playstation 2, both of which were major factors as to why I chose the I.T field in the first place. I aspire to be like the ones that first made me happy in those moments, which is why I've tried my hand at game development in the GODOT Engine, as well as learning how to put computers together using as little parts as I could find.


I've developed a massive love for tabletop games like Dungeons & Dragons, Star wars: Legion and Warhammer 40k, because they always put my brain to think in unique ways I never thought possible for myself. Strategy games and Survival horror games in general are what appeal to me the most, and I always find enjoyment in the thrill of resource management, as well as a fondness for teamwork and cooperation.


I enjoy a good strategy game, but I also love a good narrative-focused game, or even book. Very few books and genres satiate my palette for a narrative-based experience, as I'm not a fan of visual novels or reading something that doesn't particularly interest me often. But when I'm invested in a certain book or game's story, I tend to spend a never-ending amount of hours finding every ounce of detail of the world-building I can get. It's this constant flow of amazing writing and love for reading that inspired me to write for my own; I'd grown to love classes involving english and writing.
